About us

Flying Blue Tours and Travel Limited, is a owner managed company that has grown over the years into one of East Africa’s leading Travel Agency, we have striven to make travel simple, worry-free, and fun for our corporate and leisure customers alike. Your complete satisfaction is only the first of our goals.
We are gradually growing to become an established and popular company with an excellent track record for the best customer satisfaction.
We have never compromised on the quality of services provided to the customer. We believe in keeping the customers happy and providing them with products and packages at a very competent prices. We have an excellent staff team who will guide you with their best ideas by keeping in constant touch with your organization and informing about the market trends.
